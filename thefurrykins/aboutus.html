Our Mission

                             "Being pet owners ourselves, we know the pain and trauma we undergo while boarding the furries into a kennel.So we have come up with an idea that all the furry mates could be cared of during the owner's absence and also give the "furry kins" an awesome experience"




A little bit more about us!!

It is said that 'until you have met and had a pet, you do not know what true love is'.We also know how painful it is for the pet owners to drop off their furries in any kennel and keep watchful and thinking of them. Many people place their pets with their friends.But here we want to enable the pet owners to connect to the whole world of pet lovers who can handle their pets exactly like their own.


Why choose us??


24/7 Availability

Would you like to talk more? Still have questions? Need help making a reservation, or have a unique situation? We are available via telephone, email or even chat whenever you need it. Contact us anytime!


Get Daily Updates

Every pet parent receives daily photo updates, videos, and messages about their dog. You will see how much your fur baby is enjoying its stay in the safety of our caring hands. If you wish, we can also FaceTime to allow you a more direct opportunity to check in with your doggie. So even if you are far away, out of town, it will always feel like you are near your pet…. all the time!
